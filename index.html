About Us

I believe we can all find some experiences in life difficult and that taking the first steps towards counselling or therapy can be tough. I will help make the process less daunting and work through any issues you may be experiencing. I see clients face to face in private and confidential rooms, or provide walking therapy , telephone counselling and online counselling (via Zoom) sessions are also available.


My name is Cherry Baguneid and I am a psychotherapist/counsellor who work’s in a person-centred way, offering and holding a safe, non-judgemental space for you to explore what is going on in your world. I offer individual or group counselling and I can see clients either weekly or fortnightly. The issues I can help you with range from anxiety, depression, loss, relationship, family or work issues, domestic violence/sexual abuse and general life difficulties. I have specialised in dealing with clients who are experiencing long term and/or life-limiting diseases, those caring for their loved-one’s and also with clients who are struggling with coming to terms with bereavement. I have facilitated bereavement groups for several years.

Fees and Availability

There is no charge for the initial session which lasts 30 minutes. This gives us the opportunity to discuss what you feel you need from your therapy and for us to see if we could work together. If you decide you would like to work with me, I charge £50.00 for each 50 minute session. A concessionary rate can be negotiated if you are unwaged (students or unemployed).
